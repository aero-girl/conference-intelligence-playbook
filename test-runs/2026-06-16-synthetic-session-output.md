## For technical AI consultants

- Delivery implication: build reusable agent production patterns, not bespoke demos.
- Architecture implication: logging, evaluation, permissions, fallback paths, and human escalation must be designed upfront.
- Governance implication: business owners must own outcomes; central AI teams cannot be the dumping ground for every risk.
- Reusable asset idea: “agent production readiness checklist” covering data access, evals, audit logs, failure modes, security review, and rollback.
- Debate question: where should agent memory live, and who owns the risk when it is wrong?
